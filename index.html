About Me

Hello, my name is Ali, and I am a web developer passionate about new technologies. I hold a Master’s degree in New Technology and E-Commerce (Bac+4) from the École Supérieure de Commerce. My journey has allowed me to work on various projects, ranging from managing web applications to creating websites for real estate agencies.

During my internship at ASM, I participated in the development of an order management application, which helped me strengthen my skills in backend development and database management. I also created a website for a real estate agency, where I implemented advanced features for managing real estate articles.

Currently, I am working on the project **BIG TECH SOLUTIONS**, a company specializing in website creation and tailored technological solutions. This project enables me to apply my skills in Angular, Spring Boot, Laravel, as well as my knowledge in SEO and frontend development.

Beyond web development, I am passionate about technological innovation and optimizing business processes through digital solutions. My goal is to contribute to ambitious projects that combine creativity and technology.
